SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

The oxidative damage to membranes is detrimental and cannot be sufficiently controlled by antioxidants. The most susceptible membrane damage that puts cells at risk of death is in the mitochondrial membrane.

Retrotope’s drug platform, isotopically stabilized PUFAs, are unique in drug discovery. They are based on essential nutrient fats which have no pharmacological effect in the natural form. But, in the deuterated form, are sensitive and specific drugs with highly non-linear reduction of lipid peroxidation damage. Our compounds are expected to be active across various indications without any significant side effects.

about DRUG PLATFORM RACIONALE

Shchepinov filed a patent application for his potential anti-ageing invention and in 2006, together with biotech entrepreneurs Robert Molinari and Charles Cantor, he founded California-based pharma start-up Retrotope. The first thing the new company had to do was to prove the principle that isotopic substitution can extend longevity. To get some initial evidence, Shchepinov worked with collaborators to grow the roundworms Caenorhabditis elegans on isotopically enriched food to see if this increased their lifespan.

DEVELOPING
STAGE

Retrotope is in an advanced clinical stage with anticipated NDA filing in 2022. The company is poised in small and large orphan markets for immediate commercialization and there is a platform of LPO antagonist drugs being readied for larger indications.

Retrotope has begun clinical trials across 4 indications and preliminary results are overwhelmingly positive with profound safety. In early 2021, clinical trials are planned to commence with our 2nd compound RT011 for dry AMD (age-related macular degeneration) a major unmet need retinal disease indication with a TAM of more than 20M patient in US and EU.
