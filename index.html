Reinforcing the Molecules of Life
Human cells are constantly being damaged by lipid
peroxidation due to oxidative stress.
Retrotope developed a drug platform to restore damaged cells
and prevent damage.
D-PUFAs (deuterated poly-unsaturated fatty acids) are a novel approach to a previously undruggable target.

OUR
MISSION

Retrotrope is changing aging as we know it, so that people do not have to suffer the ravages of many degenerative diseases. Retrotope is leading the advance of a revolutionary new unified theory of cellular degeneration that can result in dramatically new approaches to therapy.

We are creating a new category of drugs composed of proprietary, compounds that treat degenerative diseases and improve life as we age. The first drug is being evaluated in two rare neurodegenerative diseases, Friederich’s ataxia and Infantile Neuroaxonal Dystrophy, as a rapid proof of human concept, and to support use of the drug category in other indications.
